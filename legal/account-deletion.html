Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your Acme account directly from within the mobile application. This process is simple and can be completed in just a few steps.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Open the Acme mobile app on your device.
  • Navigate to Settings (usually found in the menu or profile section).
  • Scroll down and select "Delete Account".
  • Read the confirmation message carefully.
  • Tap "Confirm" to permanently delete your account.
⚠️ Important Information
  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.
  • All your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers.
  • This action cannot be undone once completed.
  • You will lose access to all app features and any saved content.
